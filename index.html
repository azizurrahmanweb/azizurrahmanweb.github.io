About the Lab

Advancing statistical methodology for public health applications

Research Focus

TASiLab (Theoretical and Applied Statistical Innovation Lab) develops novel statistical methodologies with applications in biostatistics, public health, and data science. Our primary focus is on fast approximate inference using variational Bayes methods for intensive longitudinal data analysis.

We combine rigorous theoretical development with practical applications, working on problems ranging from functional data analysis for mortality forecasting to machine learning methods for health outcome prediction.
