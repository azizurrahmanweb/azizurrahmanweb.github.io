About TASiLab

Theoretical and Applied Statistical Innovation Laboratory

Mission

The TASi Lab is dedicated to advancing statistical science by integrating rigorous theoretical foundations with cutting-edge applied methodologies. We develop innovative statistical models and computational tools that address complex, real-world challenges across diverse domains.

Vision

To be a global leader in statistical innovation, shaping the future of data science through pioneering research and transformative applications.

Research Areas

Statistical methods at the intersection of theory and application

01

Intensive Longitudinal Data

Fast approximate Bayesian estimation using variational inference for TAR and Hidden Markov models.

02

Functional Data Analysis

FDA methods for mortality forecasting, influenza patterns, climate, and partial functional data.

03

Machine Learning in Healthcare

Interpretable ML for infant mortality, Alzheimer's prediction, and ECG-based AF detection.

04

Bayesian Modeling

Bayesian logistic regression, change point analysis, hierarchical models for public health.

05

Multi-state Markov Models

Disease progression, air pollution dynamics, blood pressure transitions.

06

Public Health Epidemiology

Maternal/child health, HIV testing, nutritional status, erosion trajectory modeling.
